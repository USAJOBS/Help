USAJOBS is now using login.gov to make signing in easier and more secure.

What do I have to do?

You will need a login.gov account to sign into USAJOBS and access your profile information.

login.gov will walk you through setting up your account, but here’s what you can expect:

  • You’ll need an email address–use the same email address you use for USAJOBS (either your primary or secondary email address).
  • You’ll need a working phone number (mobile or landline).
  • You’ll only need to create the login.gov account once.
  • You’ll use your login.gov email address and password whenever you want to sign into USAJOBS.

Once you’ve set up your login.gov account, you’ll go back to USAJOBS to finish the process.
