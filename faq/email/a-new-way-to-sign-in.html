In a couple of weeks, USAJOBS is changing the way you’ll sign in, making it easier and more secure.

What do I need to do?

For now, sign into your USAJOBS account to make sure it’s up-to-date. If everything looks okay, you don’t need to do anything else just yet.

When will the change happen?

In a couple of weeks we’ll make the change. At that time, we’ll send you another email telling you what you need to do.
